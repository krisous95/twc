Case Study: Baltimore Littering Solution

The Market Association of Baltimore City has plans to build a park in the Lexington Market area. With the mission to mitigate littering in the area, we created a hardware solution that acts an interactive trash receptacle. The proposed solution is a kiosk, where a user can dispose of virtually any type of trash item. The user follows on-screen prompts to determine how to dispose of the item properly. Research included user personas and customer journey mapping.
